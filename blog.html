December 18, 2024

The Human Initiative: People-run, AI-assisted

Let's be clear about something important: DiGiConnect is not "human-first." It's trust-fiat based.

We believe in a simple truth: Humans can't replace AI, and AI can't replace humans.

AI provides: Structure, consistency, scoring logic, and scale.
Humans provide: Judgment, context, final authority, and governance.

Trust decisions are calculated by AI but can be reviewed, corrected, or enhanced by real people. The system explicitly supports discretionary actions: manual trust bonuses, overrides, and corrections when community consensus or context justifies it.

Trust isn't automated. It's governed.

We don't remove people from the system.
We don't remove intelligence from the system.
We integrate them.

This is People-run, AI-assisted. That's the Human Initiative.
